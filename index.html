The tale of Fantastic Lyrics

The unprecedented nature of Mumford & Sons lyrics blows away the minds of millions of fans worldwide.

The finest example of their literary talents is undoubtedly White Blank Page song. There we can enjoy the most creative rhymes known such as:

  • page-rage
  • think-brink
  • attention-affection

and many others

Combined with the soulful (and prolonged) repetition of the words every other line, it creates a most wonderful experience.

You can enjoy more of these fab lyrics or share your iterpretation of them on the White Blank Page page on Genius
